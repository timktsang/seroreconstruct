# seroreconstruct: Reconstructing Antibody Dynamics to Estimate the Risk of Influenza Virus Infection

A Bayesian framework for inferring influenza infection status from
serial antibody measurements. Jointly estimates season-specific
infection probabilities, antibody boosting and waning after infection,
and baseline HAI titer distributions via MCMC. Supports multi-season
analysis and subgroup comparisons via a group_by interface. See Tsang et
al. (2022)
[doi:10.1038/s41467-022-29310-8](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-022-29310-8)
for methodological details.
